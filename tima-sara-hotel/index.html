About Us

Tima Sara Hotel is a culturally-themed boutique hotel located in the heart of a serene suburb of Tamale, the gateway to the nortern regions of Ghana.

It has a restaurant that provides authentic northern cuisine, a rooftop swimming pool, gym, and cultural event spaces
